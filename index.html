About Me

My introduction

From a young age, I was fascinated by how things work—especially when it came to technology. This curiosity naturally grew into a passion for cybersecurity. I am currently pursuing a degree in cybersecurity at college, and I have already earned certifications in Google Cybersecurity and Google IT Support. These experiences have equipped me with the skills to tackle complex challenges in the digital world. In my free time, I enjoy hands-on projects like building websites, setting up SOC home labs, performing penetration tests, and exploring new technologies using platforms like Azure, Linode, and VirtualBox. I’m committed to constant learning and adapting, striving to stay ahead in this fast-paced field.

I believe that learning is a lifelong journey. As someone who is constantly engaged in building new projects and exploring different technologies, I resonate with the idea that we are always students, continually learning and growing. Outside of my technical work, I also find joy in hiking, skating with friends, rock climbing, and reading books that help me develop good habits and stay motivated.

In addition to my professional goals, I’m focused on personal growth and contributing to the tech community. I’m passionate about sharing my knowledge through mentoring and community involvement. My aim is to blend my love for technology with a commitment to making a positive impact in both my career and personal life. I look forward to the opportunities ahead, driven by a desire to solve problems and inspire others.
